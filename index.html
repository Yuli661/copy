/ FAQ

Everything you need to know to plan a seamless and comfortable stay at Bellavista.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and are subject to availability.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer valet parking for all guests at $45 per night. Self-parking is also available in the nearby garage at a discounted rate of $35 per night.

Do you offer room service?

Room service is available daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Our menu features locally sourced ingredients and seasonal specialties.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 72 hours or more prior to arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours are subject to a one-night charge.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome dogs under 50 pounds with a non-refundable pet fee of $150 per stay. Please notify us in advance if you plan to bring a pet.

Is breakfast included?

Yes, a complimentary breakfast is included for all guests and is served daily from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM in our garden restaurant.
